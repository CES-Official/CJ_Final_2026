About the Founder
PR
Photo placeholder · Pankaj Rai

Pankaj Rai

Founder & President, COMPUDON Junior · CyberLearning Educational Society

Pankaj Rai is the founder of COMPUDON Junior and the driving force behind India's national digital citizenship championship. With over 17 years in digital education and technology literacy, he created COMPUDON Junior out of a conviction that every child deserves structured, deliberate preparation for the digital world — not just access to it.

Under his leadership, COMPUDON Junior has grown to reach 100,000+ students across 500+ institutions, spanning India and beyond. The programme is aligned to CBSE, NEP 2020, and the Microsoft Digital Literacy Standard, and is operated by CyberLearning Educational Society (CES) — the NGO arm of India CyberLearning Pvt. Ltd. — a Microsoft Global Training Partner.

He is also the co-author of Log In to Your Life — a values-meets-digital-wisdom companion book for children, offered free to every Season VII COMPUDON Junior registrant.

"We would never expect a child to drive on the highway without first learning the rules. Yet every day, we expect children to navigate the information highway with almost no preparation. That needs to change."
