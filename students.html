What you'll learn

Six skills that change how you see the internet

Online Safety

Spot fake profiles, handle cyberbullying, and know exactly what to do when something feels wrong online.

Fake News Detection

Learn to question headlines, check sources, and never fall for a fake story again. Your friends will come to you for fact-checks.

AI Literacy

Understand how AI actually works, why it sometimes gets things wrong, and how to use it without being fooled by it.

Privacy & Identity

Your data is yours. Learn what to share, what to keep private, and why your digital footprint matters more than you think.

Media Literacy

Understand how content is designed to grab your attention, and learn to choose what deserves your time.

Critical Thinking

The skill behind every other skill. Learn to slow down, ask better questions, and make decisions you're proud of.

Your journey

How it works

Register

Through your school or individually with a parent. Takes two minutes.

Prepare

Study with the COMPUDON Junior book, stories, and practice questions.

Compete

Take the championship exam. Show what you know — school, state, national, international levels.

Earn recognition

Certificates, awards, and the chance to become a COMPUDON Junior Champion.

ICCJ Ambassador Programme
