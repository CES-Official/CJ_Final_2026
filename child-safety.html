Child Safety & Data Protection

How we protect participating students data?

COMPUDON Junior serves students aged 8–14. Protecting their safety, privacy, and personal information is not an afterthought — it is a foundational design principle of everything we build.

Our commitments

Every system, process, and decision at COMPUDON Junior is guided by a simple question: would we be comfortable if this were our own child's data?

Minimum necessary data

We collect only the information required to register, participate, and receive results. No behavioural tracking, no profiling, no data monetisation.

School-mediated consent

Student registrations are processed through schools or with explicit parental consent. No child can self-register without an adult's involvement.

No third-party data sharing

Student data is never sold, rented, or shared with advertisers, data brokers, or any third party. Period.

Secure storage

All personal data is stored using industry-standard encryption and access controls. Data retention follows regulatory requirements and is reviewed annually.

Regulatory compliance

India · DPDP Act 2023

COMPUDON Junior is designed to comply with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023. This includes obtaining verifiable parental or guardian consent before processing the personal data of any child, implementing appropriate security safeguards, and honouring data principal rights including access, correction, and erasure.

United States · COPPA

For participants in the United States, COMPUDON Junior observes the requirements of the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). No personal information is collected from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent, and parents retain the right to review, modify, or delete their child's information at any time.

General principles

Regardless of jurisdiction, we apply the highest applicable standard across all countries where we operate. Where local law sets a higher bar than another region, we apply that higher bar universally. Our approach is to protect first and ask questions later — not the other way around.

What we do not do

Transparency includes being clear about what we choose not to do. COMPUDON Junior does not:

No behavioural advertising

We do not serve advertisements to children, and we do not use student data to build advertising profiles.

No algorithmic profiling

We do not use algorithms to profile, score, or categorise students beyond their championship results.

No location tracking

We do not track or store the physical location of any student at any time.

No social features

There are no chat rooms, messaging systems, or social features where students interact with unknown users.

For schools and parents

If you are a school administrator, coordinator, or parent and have questions about how student data is handled, we welcome your enquiry. We believe that trust is built through transparency, and we are happy to provide additional detail about our data practices, security measures, and compliance procedures.
