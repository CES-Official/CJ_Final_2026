2027 Digital Readiness Benchmark · Applications Open

Are your students AI ready?

Artificial intelligence is changing how students learn, create and make decisions — but how prepared are they to use it responsibly? COMPUDON Junior is inviting a limited number of schools to take part in the 2027 Digital Readiness Benchmark and find out.

Apply for your school See what's included
Grades 3–8 Up to 200 students benchmarked Free School Report No obligation to compete

Most schools have never measured how their students actually perform on AI literacy, cyber safety, media literacy and critical thinking — only assumed it. The Digital Readiness Benchmark replaces that assumption with a number, a comparison, and a plan.

What's included

A complete picture of where your students stand today

Every participating school receives the same four deliverables, at no cost, for this benchmark cohort.

Grades 3–5 Benchmarking

Up to 100 Explorers-level students assessed on age-appropriate digital readiness indicators.

Grades 6–8 Benchmarking

Up to 100 Navigators-level students assessed on the same four readiness dimensions.

School-Level Report

Scored breakdown across AI Literacy, Cyber Safety, Media Literacy and Critical Thinking — with grade-wise detail.

Principal Executive Summary

A one-page summary built for leadership review, board reporting and CBSE compliance documentation.

How it works

Three steps, no classroom disruption

1

Apply

Tell us your school and the grades you'd like benchmarked. Takes under five minutes.

2

Assess

Students complete a short, proctored online assessment — roughly 30 minutes, during a normal school period.

3

Receive Your Report

Your school-level report and Principal Executive Summary arrive within two weeks of assessment.

Two things, not one

The Benchmark and the Championship are different commitments

You can take part in the Benchmark with zero obligation to enter COMPUDON Junior. Many schools use one to decide on the other.

Digital Readiness Benchmark

Measures where your students are today. A diagnostic — free, low-effort, no public results, no ranking against other schools unless you choose to compare.

COMPUDON Junior Championship

Celebrates and recognises achievement. An annual, skills-based competition with 100+ awards across school, national and international levels.

Cohort size is limited

We're inviting a limited number of schools into this benchmark cohort

Scoring and reporting is done by hand by our assessment team, so each cohort is capped to protect turnaround time and report quality. Applications are reviewed in the order received.

Apply before the cohort closes
Data Protection

Designed for children. Built for trust.

Children's data deserves exceptional protection. Privacy, security, and responsible data handling are built into every stage of the Digital Readiness Benchmark.

Aligned with India's privacy framework

Built with consideration for the DPDP Act, CERT-In directions, and applicable MeitY guidance for student data.

Strong encryption

Industry-standard encryption protects data in transit and at rest.

Only the data we need

We collect only what's required to score and report — nothing is sold, and nothing is used for advertising.

School-controlled results

Results go to your school only. Nothing is published or shared externally without your explicit consent.
