The Curriculum

Four pillars, one connected programme

Every module returns to a single habit we call Think First — the reflex behind safe, thoughtful choices online.

Cyber Safety

Harmful interactions, digital footprint and personal-data protection — the everyday risks children actually meet.

Creativity & Critical Thinking

Problem-solving, reasoning and creativity — the durable competencies at the core of NCF 2023.

Media Literacy

Teaching students to question what they see online — spotting fake news, evaluating sources, and resisting content designed to mislead.

AI Literacy

How AI systems work, where they fail, and how to use them responsibly — aligned to CBSE AI (901).

The Complete Digital Safety and Digital Citizenship Solution for CBSE Schools

Two CBSE directives now sit on every school head's desk — Circular Skill-81/2025 making Digital Citizenship mandatory, and Circular Acad-39/2026 requiring structured Cyber Club activities and monthly Cyber Jagrookta Diwas sessions. COMPUDON Junior delivers both through a single championship — structured, age-appropriate, and zero teacher workload.
