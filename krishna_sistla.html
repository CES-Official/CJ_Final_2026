Mr. Krishna Sistla

EdTech & Oracle Veteran · Advisory Board

Mr. Krishna is a seasoned technology-and-education leader with a global footprint spanning Asia Pacific, Japan and beyond. At Oracle he served as Senior Director overseeing Oracle Academy and the Oracle Education Foundation across Asia Pacific and Japan, and as Director at Oracle University and Sun Microsystems managing education business throughout the APAC region.

He conceptualised and built Oracle's Workforce Development Program — an initiative to bridge the gap between professionals and higher-education students — which Oracle subsequently rolled out globally.

A founding member of the Executive Council of IIIT-Hyderabad, Mr. Krishna has long championed the intersection of technology and learning. His commitment to global education reform is reflected in his former role as a UNESCO Regional Expert on Transforming Education and Training Systems for Lifelong Learning in the Asia-Pacific.

A passionate advocate for 21st-century skills, he presented research at MIT's Learning International Networks Consortium (LINC) Conference in 2010 on leveraging technology to build future-ready competencies — a vision that aligns seamlessly with COMPUDON Junior's mission. He currently serves as Founding Director of TOOOPLE Technologies Pte. Ltd., a Singapore-based virtual projects company.
