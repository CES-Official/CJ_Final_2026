Data Protection

Designed for children. Built for trust.

Children's data deserves exceptional protection. Privacy, security, and responsible data handling are built into every stage of the COMPUDON Junior experience.

Aligned with India's privacy framework

Built with consideration for the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP), CERT-In directions, and applicable MeitY guidance for handling student data.

Strong encryption

Industry-standard encryption protects data in transit and at rest.

Carefully selected technology providers

We work with established technology providers that support secure handling of student data.

Only the data we need
