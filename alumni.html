Featured alumni

From championship stage to global career

Karanjot Singh
Senior Software Developer, Genesys Cloud · Toronto, Canada
India Champion 2011 · MS Word 2007 World Championship · Las Vegas Kulachi Hansraj School, New Delhi
Winning the COMPUDON India Championship in the Microsoft Word track in 2012 and representing India at the Microsoft Office Specialist World Championship in Las Vegas was a defining moment in my life. As my first trip outside India, the opportunity to represent my country while competing against the brightest minds from over 50 countries was both humbling and inspiring. Meeting people from diverse cultures on a truly global stage broadened my perspective and planted the seed for pursuing an international career.
After completing his Bachelor's in Engineering from Netaji Subhas Institute of Technology (NSIT), Delhi, Karanjot began his career at Samsung before moving to Canada to pursue a Master's in Computer Engineering at the University of Waterloo. Today, he works as a Senior Software Developer at Genesys Cloud, building cloud and AI-powered solutions for contact center technology. Along the way, he has authored IEEE research papers, won national and global hackathons, mentored aspiring innovators, and filed a patent currently under review.
More than just a competition, COMPUDON broadened my horizons, expanded my aspirations, and instilled the confidence to dream bigger. Even today, the Microsoft Office skills I honed continue to be invaluable in my professional life, while the experience itself remains one of the key milestones that shaped my academic and career journey.
