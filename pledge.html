Cyber Safety Pledge · Season VII

A commitment that begins at home.

Digital safety isn't just a school topic — it's a family habit. This pledge invites students and their families to commit together to safer, smarter, kinder behaviour online.

Aligned to CBSE Circular Acad-39/2026 · I4C / Ministry of Home Affairs Initiative

Section A

CBSE Cyber Safety Pledge

As prescribed in CBSE Circular Acad-39/2026 · Indian Cyber Crime Coordination Centre (I4C), Ministry of Home Affairs

  1. 1.I will always install mobile apps only from official websites or the Play Store.
  2. 2.I will not blindly trust social media groups or advertisements.
  3. 3.I will not trust calls from unknown numbers without verification.
  4. 4.I will always follow the principle of "Stop. Think. Then Take Action." for digital safety.
  5. 5.I will follow the Ministry of Home Affairs social media handle "Cyberdost" today and immediately.
Section B

COMPUDON Junior Additional Commitments

These commitments extend the CBSE pledge with skills from the COMPUDON Junior Season VII curriculum — six modules covering cyber safety, media literacy, critical thinking, and AI awareness.

  1. 6.Think First. Before I click, before I share, before I trust anything I see online — I will stop and think.
  2. 7.I will protect my personal information and never share passwords — not even with close friends.
  3. 8.I will speak to a trusted adult immediately if I see or experience anything online that makes me uncomfortable.
  4. 9.I will question what I read and watch online — checking sources before I believe or share anything.
  5. 10.I will use AI tools honestly, verify what they tell me, and never use them to cheat or deceive.
  6. 11.I will be kind online and will never take part in, or stay silent about, bullying or harassment of any kind.
