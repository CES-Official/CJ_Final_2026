About COMPUDON Junior

An international championship preparing children aged 8–14 for a safe, capable life online. Anchored in one principle: Think First.

COMPUDON Junior is an international championship for junior school students. Launched with inspiration from Microsoft's Digital Literacy Standard — which teaches generic ICT skills and concepts — the curriculum now also covers what children need to stay safe online, understand new technologies, and build media literacy.

The championship tests age-appropriate ICT skills vital for success in the 21st century, across three levels. The top three winning positions from each eligible participating country are invited to the International Finals, where students represent their school and their country to compete for prestigious awards — over 100 in all.

Open to students aged 8–14, preparing for the championship helps children master critical skills and thrive in the opportunities of the 21st-century global economy. Practice exams help students understand the question types and timing so they can excel.

COMPUDON Junior is brought to you by CyberLearning Educational Society (CES), also the organiser of COMPUDON — India's first-ever official championship on Microsoft Office skills, testing students on Word, Excel and PowerPoint through an online, real-time, hands-on, skill-based exam.

About CyberLearning Educational Society (CES)

CES is the NGO arm of India CyberLearning Pvt. Ltd., a Global Training Partner and Training Services Partner of Microsoft. It is a group of problem-solvers and leaders dedicated to lasting change — empowering and securing every child through digital literacy for success in the 21st-century global economy.

For more than 17 years, CES has advanced digital literacy through partnerships with over 50 Rotary Clubs in Rotary International District 3010 (later Districts 3011 & 3012), the Times Foundation, Huawei, Microsoft and Intel — supporting youth from minority and underprivileged communities with training and global-certification scholarships worth ₹2 crore.

Think First. — the philosophy underneath everything

Before you click. Before you share. Before you trust. Think First. These two words anchor every module, every lesson, and every habit COMPUDON Junior builds. Not fear — judgement. Not restriction — capability. The skill of pausing before reacting is the foundation everything else rests on.

Aligned to frameworks that matter

The COMPUDON Junior curriculum is developed with reference to internationally recognised educational standards, ensuring that what students learn is both rigorous and globally relevant:

India: NEP 2020 and NCF 2023 — aligned with the mandate for digital literacy, critical thinking, and non-academic assessment in report cards.

International: ISTE Standards for Students, UNESCO Media and Information Literacy Curriculum, UNESCO AI Competency Framework, EU DigComp 2.2, and OECD Learning Compass 2030.

Foundation: Microsoft Digital Literacy Standard — the courseware that inspired the championship's origin.

"Log In to Your Life" — the companion values book

Co-authored with Krish Dhanam (CEO, SkyLife Success — trained by Zig Ziglar), "Log In to Your Life" pairs digital-life vocabulary with the character values underneath them. COMPUDON Junior teaches digital skills. This book teaches the heart behind the habits. Think First. is the thread that connects both.
