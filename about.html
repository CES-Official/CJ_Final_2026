About the Programme

What is COMPUDON Junior?

An international digital citizenship and cyber safety championship, assessed by an independent examination, for students aged 8–14 in Grades 3–8.

Mission

Empowering Young Digital Citizens

COMPUDON Junior positions students as protectors and leaders — not victims — in digital spaces. Through assessed learning across six curriculum modules, students develop the knowledge, skills, and habits to navigate an increasingly complex online world.

The programme is built on the master tagline "Think First." — the single habit that unifies all six modules and every learning outcome.

Season VII sees the programme expanding internationally, building on six successful seasons in India.

Student Categories

Explorers & Navigators

Explorers

Grades 3–5  |  Ages 8–10

Foundation-level digital citizenship. Focused on safety, privacy basics, and recognising harmful interactions.

Navigators

Grades 6–8  |  Ages 11–14

Advanced digital citizenship. Includes AI literacy, media literacy, manipulation detection, and responsible digital leadership.

The Book

Log In to Your Life

Co-authored with values educator Krish Dhanam, Log In to Your Life is a companion guide to COMPUDON Junior — an A–Z values guide helping young people navigate life with wisdom, integrity, and purpose in the digital age.

A free digital copy is included with every Season VII student registration.
