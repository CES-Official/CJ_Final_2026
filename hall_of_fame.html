The COMPUDON Junior Hall of Fame

Champions of digital literacy — the leaders and educators whose work makes this mission possible.

Global Digital Literacy Leadership

Mr. David Saedi

Founder, Certiport Inc. · CEO, WinnowPro

Pioneer of the IC3 (Internet and Computing Core) Certification, which has helped over 3 million people across 100+ countries, and creator of world-class certifications including the Microsoft Office Specialist and Adobe Certified Associate.

Trailblazer of Digital Literacy

Mr. Sanjay Khanna

Past District Governor, Rotary International District 3011

A tireless champion of digital literacy and community service whose work has helped countless young people build the skills to thrive in a digital, competitive world.

Global Digital Literacy Leadership

Mr. Umang Das

Chief Mentor, American Tower

Five decades in the telecom industry laying the foundation for digital education in India — former Chairman of the Foreign Investors India Forum and Broadband India Forum's policy group, among many industry leadership roles.

Innovative Education Leader

Mr. Amit Saxena

VP Education, Sparsh Group of Schools, Greater Noida

A staunch advocate for cutting-edge learning solutions, working to bridge the digital divide and help students master the skills they need for the digital age.

Torch Bearer of Digital Literacy

Ms. Ritu Kaushik

Bluebells School International, New Delhi

Two-time recipient of this award. A passionate IT educator who has trained mass-media teachers on behalf of CBSE and champions true digital literacy for every student.

Torch Bearer of Digital Literacy

Ms. Ankita Yadav

Seth Anandram Jaipuria School, Lucknow

An "accidental educator" turned passionate advocate — teaching IT skills to her students and to underserved children, with work recognised by the Ministry of Education.

Torch Bearer of Digital Literacy

Ms. Mancy Agarwal

Seth Anandram Jaipuria School, Ghaziabad

An educator of 15+ years and MIEE & ACE-certified teacher, committed to helping children become innovative, confident, collaborative lifelong learners.

Innovative Education Leader

Dr. Pratibha Kohli

Maharaja Agrasen Model School, New Delhi

Under her leadership her school won the first-ever World Champion Award in PowerPoint. A CBSE and State Teacher Award recipient, ATAL Tinkering Lab Mentor of Change, and two-time Educationist of the Year.

Innovative Education Leader

Mr. Mohd Faran Shamsi

City Montessori Schools, Lucknow

Head of E-Learning & Digital Enablement, finding innovative ways to put technology to work for students at one of the world's largest schools.
