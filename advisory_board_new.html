Academic & Policy Advisory Board

The people who keep COMPUDON Junior credible

Leaders from cybersecurity, education policy, EdTech and academia guide the programme's rigour and direction.

AN

Prof. (Dr.) A.K. Nayak

Technology & AI Advisor

Pro-Chancellor of the International University, Nagaland, with over 41 years in Computer Science and Management. A former Director and Professor at IIBM, Patna, he has earned national and international honours for education and IT — including the Abdul Kalam Gold Medal Award.

DW

Mr. Drew Williams

Cybersecurity & Defense Expert

A U.S. Navy veteran who began as a Defense Information Specialist tracking Soviet naval movements (1979–82). He launched one of the industry's first Host Intrusion Detection systems and an early leading SIEM tool, and co-founded Symantec's SWAT security-research team.

Know more
KS

Mr. Krishna Sistla

EdTech & Oracle Veteran

A global technology-and-education leader who led Oracle Academy and the Oracle Education Foundation across Asia Pacific and Japan, and directed education business at Oracle University and Sun Microsystems. Founding Director of TOOOPLE Technologies, Singapore.

Know more
LP

Dr. Laurence Peters

Education Policy Advisor

Adjunct Associate Professor at the Johns Hopkins School of Education and former senior policy advisor at the U.S. Department of Education. A former Counsel to the U.S. House Subcommittee on Select Education, and author on the United Nations and global education reform.

Know more
UD

Mr. Umang Das

Telecom & Broadband Policy Advisor

A telecom veteran with over 50 years in the industry, Umang Das chairs the Broadband India Forum's Policy and Regulatory Group and previously led Spice Telecom, the company behind India's first mobile call in 1995. An active Rotarian, he drives digital literacy and skill-building initiatives with partners including Microsoft and NSDC.

Content & Curriculum

The voice behind our content

A separate discipline from policy and technology — the clinical and ethical lens that shapes how we talk to children.

AG

Aashi Gaba

Neuropsychologist & Content Advisor

A neuropsychologist specialising in how digital environments shape children's cognitive and emotional development, Aashi brings clinical rigour and AI-ethics expertise to COMPUDON Junior's content. She shapes frameworks that build resilience and critical thinking, translating complex technology into guidance that is scientifically grounded, psychologically sensitive, and trusted by schools, parents and children alike.
