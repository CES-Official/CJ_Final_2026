Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Who gets Regional Awards?

The top three students in each category (Explorers and Navigators) from each of the six regions — East, West, North, South, Central, and North East — receive Regional Champion, Silver, and Bronze awards.

Who gets National Awards?

The highest-scoring students across all of India compete for the National Champion title, along with National Silver and National Bronze in each category.

How are ties resolved?

In the event of a tie, the student who completed their assessment in less time is ranked higher. If the tie persists, both students receive the same award.

Is there a minimum participation requirement?

There is no minimum number of students required from a school. Even a single student can participate and win awards.

Why is there no Global Champion title?

COMPUDON Junior recognises National Champions from each participating country equally. There is no single "World Champion" — instead, every National Champion earns a permanent place in the Global Champions Gallery, celebrating excellence across nations without ranking countries against each other.

What is the Global Champions Gallery?

The Global Champions Gallery is a permanent online record of every National Champion from every participating country. It includes the student's name, school, country, and year — creating a lasting legacy that grows every season.
