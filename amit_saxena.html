Dr. Amit Saxena

Education Strategist & Institution Builder · Advisory Board

With nearly three decades of leadership across K-12 education, higher education, and corporate learning, Dr. Amit Saxena is recognised as one of India's leading institution builders and education strategists. As CEO – Education at Sparsh Universe, he leads a growing ecosystem of schools and higher education institutions ranked among India's leading educational organisations by Times of India, EducationWorld, and Careers360, with accreditations including NABET, NBA, NAAC (A+), and QS I-GAUGE Gold.

He developed the KG to Corporate philosophy and the 3C Model — Character, Competencies, and Careerism — to connect school, higher education, and industry into one path to lifelong success. Over the past decade, he has led the growth of institutions including Sparsh Global School, Sparsh Global Business School, and Sparsh Academy, and launched IRIS, India's first AI-powered Robo Teacher initiative.

Beyond institution building, he contributes to education policy as a Charter Member of TiE, a member of FICCI ARISE, and an Ambassador for HundrED, Finland's global education innovation network, with international collaborations spanning the United Nations, WHO, INSEAD, and NUS.

A respected thought leader, he curates the Kafila Lit Fest and hosts the Sparsh Rubaru podcast, and was named Outstanding CEO of the Year at the Global Leadership Summit, Oxford University, 2025. He serves on the COMPUDON Junior Academic and Policy Advisory Board in alignment with its focus on rigorous, future-ready education.
