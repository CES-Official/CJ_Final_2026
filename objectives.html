Season VII Syllabus

Four pillars. Twelve modules. One future-ready child.

Explore what students master this season. Syllabus depth is tuned to age — Grades 3–5 and Grades 6–8 follow the same pillars at different levels.

Pillar 01

Cyber Safety

The everyday risks children actually meet online — and how to handle them.

M1

Harmful interactions & exploitation

Recognising and managing harmful interactions and exploitation risks.

M2

Resisting manipulation

Resisting manipulation and critically evaluating digital content.

M3

Privacy & digital identity

Protecting personal privacy and managing a digital identity.

Pillar 02

Creativity & Critical Thinking

The durable competencies at the core of NCF 2023.

M4

Creativity across subjects

Applying creativity to enhance learning across every subject.

M5

Designing how to learn

Designing effective, engaging learning strategies.

M6

Thinking beyond scores

Assessing and improving thinking skills beyond academic marks.

Pillar 03

Media Literacy

India ranks #1 in the world for misinformation risk. This is the antidote.

M7

Spotting disinformation

Identifying and analysing disinformation in the wild.

M8

Building resistance

Understanding susceptibility and building resistance to misinformation.

Pillar 04

AI Literacy

Aligned to CBSE AI (901): how AI works, where it fails, and how to use it well.

M9

AI systems & limits

Understanding AI systems and their limitations.

M10

Data fundamentals

Applying data fundamentals in AI contexts.

M11

Using AI responsibly

Using AI tools effectively and responsibly.

M12

Impact & ethics

Evaluating the impact and ethics of AI.
